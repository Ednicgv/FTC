Activities

Hiking

Rustic Yurts has 10 miles of hiking trails and is adjacent to a state part. Go it alone or join one of the many guided hikes.

Kayaking

Ocean kayaks are available for rental.


Bird Watching

Rustic Yurts offers guided bird watching trips at sunrise and sunset throughout the week.
