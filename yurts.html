Yurts

What is a Yurt?
Our Yurts are permanent structures four feet of the ground. Each Yurt has reinforced canvas walls, a wooden floor, and a domed roof.

How are Yurts furnished?
Each Yurt is furnished with a queen-sized bed with down comforter and gas-fired stove. The camping experience also includes electricity and a sink with running water. Shower and restrooms facilities are located inside the lodge.



What should I bring?
Pack comfortable walking shoes and plan for changing weather with layers of clothes. Having a sense of adventure is also a necessity.
